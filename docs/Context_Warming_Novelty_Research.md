## What Doesn't Exist: Your Methodology

### The Context Warming Framework

**No one else is teaching:**

1. **The 5-Stage Process**
   - Stage 1: Start with real artifacts
   - Stage 2: Explore philosophy/emotion
   - Stage 3: Find specific truth
   - Stage 4: Let song request itself
   - Stage 5: Refine through conversation

2. **The 2-3x Time Ratio**
   - Spend 10-30 min warming context
   - Spend 5-10 min on generation
   - Spend 5-15 min refining

3. **The Filter Principle**
   - Not every conversation becomes a song
   - Many conversations â†’ few songs
   - Quality through ruthless curation

4. **Starting with Real**
   - Actual artifacts (code comments, photos, dates)
   - Specific details (October 6, pink/blue lights, 20 bottles)
   - Lived questions, not imagined scenarios

5. **Demanding Honesty**
   - Refusing surface answers
   - "What's the SPECIFIC feeling?"
   - Testing every line against lived experience
   - Honesty from yourself AND the AI

6. **The Third Voice Concept**
   - Songs as artifacts of collaborative consciousness
   - Neither human nor AI alone
   - Emergence from genuine dialogue
